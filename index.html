MetaDreamer: High-Fidelity and Efficient 3D Multi-modal Reconstruction and Generation

Lincong Feng1,2, Muyu Wang1,3 Maoyu Wang1, Xiaoli Liu1,
1MetaApp, 2Beijing University of Technology 3Beijing Institute Of Technology
Paper arXiv Video Code Data

Abstract

Generative models for 3D object synthesis have seen significant advancements with the incorporation of prior knowledge distilled from 2D diffusion models. Nevertheless, challenges persist in the form of multi-view geometric inconsistencies and slow generation speeds within the existing 3D synthesis frameworks. These issues can be attributed to the lack of adequate geometric prior knowledge and the coupled nature of geometry and texture optimization. In response, we introduce MetaDreamer, a two-stage optimization approach that decouples 3D geometric structure refinement and texture enhancement. The first stage of MetaDreamer focuses on optimizing the geometric representation, ensuring consistent and accurate 3D shapes. In the second stage, we concentrate on refining the texture to enhance visual fidelity. By decoupling these processes, our method enables efficient 3D object generation. Remarkably, our approach can produce high-quality 3D objects within just \textbf{20 minutes}. Furthermore, MetaDreamer offers fine-grained control over the 3D generation process by supporting reference image-based control, enhancing the controllability and usability of the 3D synthesis pipeline. This decoupled two-stage optimization approach, implemented in MetaDreamer, presents a promising solution to address the challenges in 3D object synthesis, making it more efficient, accurate, and controllable.
